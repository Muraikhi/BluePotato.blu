About

One hundred and fifty trillion years ago, there was a creature known as "Batos." It belonged to a species called "Blue Potatoes." Batos was an extraordinarily powerful being, the only one capable of self-replication. As a result, new beings appeared, one of which was called "Potato Man" and the other "Potato Woman," who were the reason for the existence of the Blue Potatoes. Potato Man and Potato Woman were among the strongest creatures in the universe, thanks to their immense physical, mental, and technological power. They possessed vast knowledge in all fields of the universe and lived on the Blue Planet in a Blue Dimension, billions of light-years away from Earth.
